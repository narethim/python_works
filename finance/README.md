## Get Data From Brokerage

### Vanguard

1. Log in to Vanguard.
2. Go to "My Accounts" > "Transaction history".
3. Click the "Download" button.
4. Under "Step 1", select "A spreadsheet-compatible CSV file".
5. Select your desired date range (up to 18 months) and accounts.
6. Click "Download" and import the CSV file into your software.
